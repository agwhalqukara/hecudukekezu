Meet Rhona Gemmell, The Ex-Wife Of Robbie Coltrane

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 19, 2024

Rhona Gemmell was the ex-wife of the famous actor Robbie Coltrane. Robbie had recently died, but he is popularly known for his role as Hagrid in the all-time great movie Harry Potter. 

The movie had several sequels where Robbie solely played Hagrid’s role because of the natural persona he brought into the character. 

Through his role in the movies, he got attention from children aged 9 to 15. 

After the death of Robbie, the fans were curious to know about Hagrid’s love life and relationship with his ex-wife.

Below, the article will cover everything about who is Robbie Coltrane’s Ex-Wife, Rhona Gemmell. It will also share information about Robbie’s children and why Rhona Gemmell and Robbie got divorced.

Who is Robbie Coltrane’s Ex-Wife, Rhona Gemmel?

In December 1988, the late James Bond star, Robbie Coltrane, met his first wife, Rhona Gemmell. It was the time when both were in college. 

Rhona was studying while Robbie’s career took a leap of faith. He started to receive many roles in different movies, including the role of James Bond. 

In 10 year period, 1998, Robbie gained a lot of popularity in the entertainment industry. Rhona Gemmell had studied sculpture, so she took it as her career. She was also enrolled as a pilates and wellness teacher.

Progressing in her career, she was in contact with pilates-focused health studios such as Body Control Pilates and Otago Street Pilates.

She made her career in London, where she received many achievements. They included award certifications in Body Control Master Matwork and Comprehensive Studio.

Why did Robbie and Rhona split?

Despite Robbie and Rhona meeting each other for the first time in 1988, the couple tied the knot after 11 years. 

On 11 December 1999, the couple made it official that they were married. Prior to their marriage, they had been seen on a number of occasions together. 

However, in 2003, the couple officially announced their divorce. Hagrid’s fans said this was an unfortunate incident they heard about their favorite character’s life.

Daily Mail was told by Robbie’s publicist, “It’s true they have separated. It is amicable, and there’s no one else involved.”

He added, “They are apart and it’s sad. Robbie’s work takes him away an awful lot and maybe the age difference was playing a part too.”

Do Robbie and Rhona have children?

Robbie and Rhona had become parents for the first time in 1992. They were blessed with a son who they named Spencer. 

After six years, the couple became parents for the second time when they welcomed their daughter Alice in 1998. 

On 14 October 2022, agent Belinda Wright shared with the public that the famous actor Robbie Coltrane had passed away. 

She said, “He will probably be best remembered for the next decades as Hagrid in the Harry Potter films. A role that which joy to children and adults alike all worldwide, prompting a stream of fan letters every week for more than twenty years.”
