Reese Witherspoon cant live without this eye cream when she travels

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 21, 2024

You'll want to try it asap.

    When Reese Witherspoon is prepping for a trip, she makes sure to keep a few beauty products in her suitcase that keep her looking fresh upon arrival and during her getaway, and she's revealed three of her faves.
